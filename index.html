About FeedBridge

FeedBridge is a nonprofit organization dedicated to reducing food waste and hunger in our community. We connect individuals and businesses with surplus food to local shelters, food banks, and families in need.

Founded in 2023, we've already redistributed over 50,000 meals to those facing food insecurity. Our network of volunteers ensures that good food doesn't go to waste while neighbors go hungry.
